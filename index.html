About My Mission

My name is Batoul Nehme, and I am a sophomore student who wants to create a cooking guide that is easy to follow for women who may not have access to education or who may struggle with reading.

Many traditional cookbooks rely on long written instructions that can be difficult for some people to understand. My goal with the Visual Cooking Guide is to make recipes accessible by focusing on pictures, simple ingredients, and short measurements.

Through this project, I hope to help uneducated and illiterate women learn how to cook meals from different cultures around the world in a simple and visual way.

Cooking can empower women by giving them independence, confidence, and new opportunities in their daily lives.

Message to Young Ladies

To every young woman reading this: believe in your ability to learn and grow. Skills like cooking are powerful because they allow you to take care of yourself and your family.

Your creativity, strength, and determination can shape your future. Never stop learning and believing in your potential.
